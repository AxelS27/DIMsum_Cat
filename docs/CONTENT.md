# DIMsum Cat — Content Strategy

Content is organized into 4 main categories. Each becomes a folder under `content/`.

---

## 1. Story (연애 스토리)

Relatable love/crush mini-stories told through DIMsum Cat's perspective. Short dramatic beats with Korean text + English subtitles.

**Vibe:** emotional, relatable, slightly chaotic, "this is literally me"

**Format:**
- 15–25 seconds
- 6–9 beats
- TTS voice narrating each beat
- Soft pastel background
- Background music: lo-fi, soft romantic

**Example stories:**
- `story_crush` — he talked to me first at school!!
- `story_read` — left me on read... 3 days ago
- `story_confession` — I finally confessed
- `story_friendzone` — just as friends... 🙂
- `story_ghosted` — he left and never came back
- `story_extext` — my ex just texted "hey"

**Animations to use:** normal_talk, want_attention, shy_blush, embarrassed, side_eye, determined, cheering, crying, hugging_pillow, shocked, whisper_secret

---

## 2. POV (POV 시리즈)

POV-format content. DIMsum Cat acts as the viewer or the subject. Text sets up the scenario, character reacts.

**Vibe:** immersive, funny, dramatic, trending format

**Format:**
- 10–18 seconds
- 4–6 beats
- Text starts with "POV:" setup
- Reaction escalates beat by beat
- TTS optional (reaction sounds work too)

**Example ideas:**
- `pov_dating_kpop_fan` — POV: you're dating a K-pop fan
- `pov_crush_texts` — POV: your crush texts you first
- `pov_mom_finds_out` — POV: your mom finds your search history
- `pov_exam_tomorrow` — POV: exam is tomorrow and you haven't studied
- `pov_first_date` — POV: it's your first date and you're nervous

**Animations to use:** shocked, nervous_sweat, peek_from_box, side_eye, laughing_hard, tiny_tantrum, startled_jump, mischief_grin

---

## 3. K-pop (케이팝 시리즈)

DIMsum Cat reacts to or "performs" viral K-pop moments. Background music is the actual K-pop track. Cat dances/vibes to the drop or chorus.

**Vibe:** hype, fan energy, addictive loop

**Format:**
- 10–20 seconds
- Timed to the K-pop song's viral moment (drop, chorus, ad-lib)
- Minimal text — let the music carry it
- Korean fangirl captions ("언니 이 파트!!!!")
- No TTS — music IS the audio
- Loop the best 4-second animation segment on the beat drop

**Example ideas:**
- `kpop_aespa_supernova` — synced to Supernova drop
- `kpop_newjeans_hype` — NewJeans "Hype Boy" chorus
- `kpop_bts_dynamite` — BTS Dynamite hook
- `kpop_ive_baddie` — IVE "Baddie" moment
- `kpop_stayc` — STAYC "ASAP" bounce

**Animations to use:** mini_twirl, cheering, happy_bounce, happy_wiggle, clapping, finger_heart, sassy_pose, blow_kiss

**Note:** Use `"tts": false` — bg_music is the K-pop track, volume at full.

---

## 4. Korean Tutorial (한국어 배우기)

Teach one Korean word, phrase, or expression per video. DIMsum Cat explains it in a cute, memorable way.

**Vibe:** educational but cute, "learning without trying"

**Format:**
- 12–20 seconds
- 4–6 beats
- Korean word/phrase displayed large
- Pronunciation + meaning in English subtitle
- Example sentence or context beat
- Ends with DIMsum Cat using it naturally

**Example ideas:**
- `korean_aegyo` — 애교 (aegyo) — acting cute
- `korean_fighting` — 화이팅! — let's go / fighting spirit
- `korean_jeongmal` — 정말? — really?
- `korean_jinja` — 진짜 — seriously / for real
- `korean_heol` — 헐 — OMG / no way
- `korean_nunchi` — 눈치 — reading the room
- `korean_loveyou` — 사랑해 vs 좋아해 — I love you vs I like you
- `korean_bbang` — 빵 터졌어 — I burst out laughing

**Animations to use:** normal_talk, curious_peek, head_tilt, finger_heart, wink, shy_blush, laughing_hard, listening_closely

## 5. K-Drama Recreate (드라마 재현)

DIMsum Cat recreates iconic or viral scenes from Korean dramas. Fans instantly recognize the moment and share it — huge potential for organic reach.

**Vibe:** dramatic, nostalgic, "omg this scene", fan service

**Format:**
- 15–25 seconds
- 5–8 beats
- Opens with drama title + episode context as subtitle
- DIMsum Cat acts out the scene beat by beat
- Korean dialogue (original or paraphrased) as main text
- English subtitle for non-Korean audience
- Background music: OST from the drama or emotional ballad

**Example ideas:**
- `kdrama_goblin_confession` — Goblin "도깨비" confession scene
- `kdrama_crash_landing` — Crash Landing on You "사랑해요" moment
- `kdrama_boys_over_flowers` — Jun-pyo chasing Jan-di
- `kdrama_my_love_from_star` — "별에서 온 그대" ending
- `kdrama_reply_1988` — Deok-sun choosing Choi Taek
- `kdrama_strong_woman` — Bong-soon and Min-hyuk cute moment
- `kdrama_queen_tears` — Hae-in's tearful scene (2024 viral)
- `kdrama_my_demon` — Do-hee & Gu-won contract scene

**Animations to use:** shy_blush, heart_eyes, whisper_secret, blow_kiss, crying, hugging_pillow, determined, shocked, need_a_hug, pleading, wink

**Tips:**
- Use the actual drama OST as `bg_music` for instant recognition
- Keep dialogue short — 1 line per beat max
- End on the most iconic/emotional line of the scene
- Add drama title as first beat subtitle for context
